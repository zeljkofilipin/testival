[Meetup](https://www.meetup.com/SoftwareTestingClub/events/136690002/) (2)

Zagreb STC #12 meetup held at Zip.Factory.

The group planned to analyze StarCraft 2 Starter Edition by watching a gameplay overview video. Participants were instructed to come to session with pencil and notebook.

The testing session involved two phases: first, attendees would gather game requirements by watching gameplay overview video and create a mind map. Subsequently, they would take game play introduction campaigns, and will expand our mind map.

Session Duration: 60 minutes

The core purpose was teaching testers how to listen and ask questions, which is fundamental skill of every software tester.

Pizza and refreshments were provided. Sponsor: Toptal.
