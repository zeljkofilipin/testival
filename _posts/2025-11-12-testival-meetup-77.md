Main Event (1h 30min)

18:15 DECODE Introduction

18:35 Sanjin Grahovar Sadiković (DECODE): Maestro: A Modern Take on Mobile Test Automation

Maestro brings a fresh perspective to mobile test automation. This session dives into how this open source framework simplifies testing, improves speed, and empowers teams to focus on building rather than debugging. Expect hands-on insights and practical examples you can take back to your next project.

19:15 Greg Paskal: AI Insights for the Quality Minded.

With over 40 years of experience in testing and automation, Greg Paskal shares invaluable insights with firsthand knowledge working with AI tools from Copilot, Gemini, and Claude. From the development of his AI collaboration methodology to the challenges of integrating AI tools into real-world development projects, you are sure to come away with some practical insights from this talk.
