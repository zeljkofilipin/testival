![](http://testival.diotref.com/wp-content/uploads/2010/04/een.jpg "een")Enterprise Europe Network, EEN, is backing up business event on the conference ViaQA 10. With slogan "Business Support on Your Doorstep" one of goals of EEN is to match small and medium enterprises across Europe. One of the meeting places for SME business owners and representatives is ViaQA 10. EEN is hoping to make this conference a meeting point of software testing services providers and software vendors. See more info about conference on [Croatian EEN site](http://een.hr/default.asp?kalendar/4.6.2010/prvi-poziv-na-konferenciju-%84viaqa-10--a-software-testers%60-event/111) and [official EEN site](http://www.enterprise-europe-network.ec.europa.eu/public/calendar/viewdetails.cfm?EventID=2364&type=future). If you find the ViaQA 10 business event interesitng, please do register and submit your company's profile so EEN can match you with potential partners. Or you can see list of businesses that will be presenting their services or products? Maybe there is a potential business partner there. Request a meeting.
