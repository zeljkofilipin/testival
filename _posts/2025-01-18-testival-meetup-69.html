= Regular talks (10-30 minutes each)

Talk 1: Viktor Kvaternjak, Principal Engineer in Test, ReversingLabs

How Quality Assurance helps build Secure Software?

Building great software that is also secure is not easy. Software development is a complex, ever-changing process and new threats are constantly emerging in the wild. We will analyze several recent high-profile cybersecurity incidents and explore how QA can help make software less vulnerable and more resilient, prevent tampering and keep code free of malware, while at the same time ensuring regulatory compliance and safety of the secrets.
