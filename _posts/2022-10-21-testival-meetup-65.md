[Meetup](https://www.meetup.com/testival/events/288873875/)

It's time for another meetup. This time our host will be Cinnamon.

Introduction (about 10 minutes total)

At the beginning of every meetup, we like to get to know each other better. Prepare to say a sentence or two about yourself.

Regular talks (10-30 minutes each)

#1 Danijel Popović (Cinnamon) - Maven integration with Selenium

There is no real Selenium project without Maven. A brief introduction to Maven, what is it, how to set up maven in a Selenium project, and why how to use it to optimize the execution of our test cases.

#2 Noah Jelich - Test-driven Blockchain Development

Really, the only way it should be done. A brief introduction to testing blockchain smart contracts, from choosing the right buildchains to the significance of coverage when it comes to security assessments.

Lightning talks (5 minutes each)

Short 5 minutes talks that anyone can apply for at the event.

Sponsor

Cinnamon

After the meetup

We will continue the conversation at Cinnamon with snacks and drinks.
