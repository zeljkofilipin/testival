[Meetup](https://www.meetup.com/SoftwareTestingClub/events/69947212/) (3)

We are proudly announcing 5th Zagreb Software Testing Club meetup. Meet up will be held on 19th July, Thursday, from 17.30 in Palagruza conference room at Ericsson Nikola Tesla, Krapinska 45.

The gathering welcomes software testers and developers interested in the testing field. Two presenters -- Zeljko and Karlo -- plan to deliver a 20-minute talk titled "Test like dr. House," which they previously showcased at the Webstrategija 14 conference.

Lightning talk proposals:

- Game of Set
- Testers certification
- Weekend Testing Croatia
- Exploratory testing

Ericsson Nikola Tesla is venue sponsor and Asseco SEE is the sponsor for pizza and beverages.
