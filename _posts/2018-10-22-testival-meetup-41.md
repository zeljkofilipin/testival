[Meetup](https://www.meetup.com/testival/events/255357139/)

For Testival Meetup #41 we have invited a special guest - Michael Bolton.

To software testers, Michael needs no introduction, but let us say that, with James Bach, he co-authored Rapid Software Testing methodology, he is a regular speaker at the largest software testing conferences around the world and contributor to software testing books and magazines.

We are very excited about having Michael in our community.

On the meetup, Michael will give a talk and there will be room for Q&A and networking.

This meetup is organized in cooperation with Change Con and Zagreb Innovation Centre - ZICER that is giving you an opportunity to learn from the world-class expert.

Note that the number of seats is limited to 30 public attendees whereas the 40 seats are reserved for Innovation Centre residents.
