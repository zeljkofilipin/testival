[Meetup](https://www.meetup.com/testival/events/239482386/)

Regular talks (10-30 minutes)

#1 Zoran Majstorović, ButterflyMX: Ruby Test Suite (18:15-18:45)

Overview of the standard Ruby test suite including RSpec, Capybara, FactoryGirl, Simplecov and few more.

#2 Marko Filipin, Nova runda brewery: A/B testing beer (19:00-19:30)

For technical reasons, there are always small differences between batches of the same beer. The goal is to make the difference so small that the vast majority of the people do not notice them. After a short introduction everybody will receive 3 beer samples. 2 of them will be from the same batch, 1 will be from a different one. The goal of the talk will be to see how many people have guessed correctly.
