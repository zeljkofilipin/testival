[![Photo by len-k-a](http://testival.diotref.com/wp-content/uploads/2010/03/girl-laptop.jpg?w=150 "Photo by len-k-a ")](http://testival.diotref.com/wp-content/uploads/2010/03/girl-laptop.jpg)A question arose by some of you: who this conference is for? Ok, it seems it is not obvious :) The ["About"](http://testival.diotref.com/about/)page says that the conference is intended to be a meeting point for people involved in software testing (and software quality assurance). Since all software users do test software in some extent, simply by using it and thinking of it, it is also meant to be a place for people who does not necessarily professionally work as testers but are interested in this subject. So all of you which feel like you can find something interested in our growing [programme](http://testival.diotref.com/programme/) are welcomed. Our presenters will have in mind that potentially wide audience is attending the conference, so will restrain of being too technical. No one wants people to think software testing is not a cool and interesting profession :)
