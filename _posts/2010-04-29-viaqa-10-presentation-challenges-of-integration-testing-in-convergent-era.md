Why? Today, you need only your mobile phone and you can watch media, shop, determine your position and nearby, well whatever you need nearby – shop, hotel, restaurant, pharmacy, besides just talking and messaging, as not so many years ago. Integration testing became much more challenging, especially taking into consideration that, pushed by the competition, time-to-market is shrinking all the time. Come to ViaQA 10 and hear integration expert, Stanislav Stresnjak from Siemens talking about his experiences in this demanding software testing field.
