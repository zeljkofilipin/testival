  • prof. dr. sc. Zdravko Krakar: Quality and maturity of the IT profession, and different IT views (IT as practice; IT in business systems; IT in Croatia) - 30 minutes
  • doc. dr. sc. Melita Kozina: SPI (Software Process Improvement) in software development process - 20 minutes
  • doc. dr. sc. Valentina Kirini: Software quality norms - 20 minutes
  • mr. sc. Nadica Hrgarek: Software verification and validation best practices - 20 minutes
  • prof. dr. sc. Zdravko Krakar (chair): Discussion - 30 minutes
