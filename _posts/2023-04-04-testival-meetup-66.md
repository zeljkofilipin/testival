[Meetup](https://www.meetup.com/testival/events/292252623/)

It's time for the first Testival meetup in 2023!

This time the meetup is hosted by RBA who have expressed their desire in supporting the QA and Testing community.

Introduction (about 10 minutes total)

At the beginning of every meetup, we like to get to know each other better. Prepare to say a sentence or two about yourself.

Regular talks (10-30 minutes each)

#1 Ivan Horvat (RBA) - Testing A Legacy Application (What google doesn't know)

How to automate a legacy app that is hard to test using the standard approach. We will show an example of our testing process, explain problems and discuss solutions. Ivan is an IT veteran with 20+ years of experience. Automating everything, from daily tasks for end users to application tests.

#2 Vatroslav Mileusnić (Tabu.hr) - State of QA salaries from TABU data

After saying a few words about TABU and its relevancy, we'll look into what the data says about salaries for the QA position. Vatroslav is TABU Product Lead who has been promoting salary transparency for over 10 years.

Lightning talks (5 minutes each - if we have time)

Short 5 minutes talks that anyone can apply for at the event.

Sponsor

RBA

After the meetup

We will continue the conversation at RBA with snacks and drinks.
