[Meetup](https://www.meetup.com/testival/events/286034317/)

Testival is back!

After the uncertainty of the pandemic, we are finally restarting our regular meetups.

Introduction (about 10 minutes total)

At the beginning of every meetup, we like to get to know each other a bit better. Prepare to say a sentence or two about yourself.

Regular talks (10-30 minutes each)

#1 Vesna Bilić (Photomath) - Excel in your QA career!

The majority of QA Engineers that I've met throughout my career started testing by the accident. I'm Vesna, Engineering Manager in Photomath's QA Team and I'll talk about QA career options and what you can do to excel.

#2 Niko Mađar (Photomath) - Talk from .debug - How programs test programs

Niko will present his talk from this year's .debug conference. It will cover how test automation works, the most common pitfalls, and his experience in Photomath so far as well as addressing the question of how to test machine learning systems.

Lightning talks (5 minutes each)

Short 5 minutes talks that anyone can apply for at the event.
