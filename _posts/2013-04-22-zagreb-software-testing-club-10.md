We are proud to announce Zagreb Software Testing Club #10 meetup! Meetup will be held in ZIP.Factory, Remetinecka cesta 7, 1000 Zagreb, on next Monday, 22.4. We are starting sharp on 17.30. Why? Because we have our first official international software testing guest, Gojko Adzic! Gojko is the author of "Specification by Example" and you will have a chance to ask him questions about modern software development and testing. Traditional pizza and beverages will be sponsored by Tentamen.
