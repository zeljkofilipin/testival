Regular talks (10-30 minutes each)

#1 Niko Madjar (Lemax) - How to test with limited resources / getting meaningful results fast and planning for failure.

Niko will demonstrate how to efficiently and quickly setup good test plan, organise the team and gather valuable test results in shortage of time and resources.

#2 Alexander Todorov (Kiwi) - Kiwi TCMS: past, present & future - an introduction to an open source test case management system

Alexander is a senior QA engineer from Sofia, Bulgaria and open source Python hacker with 12+ years of experience. He loves everything open source, public speaking, cooking with wine and riding fast motorcycles! Alexander is the project lead behind Kiwi TCMS -- the leading open source test case management system.
