[Meetup](https://www.meetup.com/testival/events/262265851/)

Introduction (about 10 minutes total)

At the beginning of every meetup, we like to get to know each other a bit better. Prepare to say a sentence or two about yourself.

Regular talks (10-30 minutes each)

#1 Frane Rogosic (Deegloo): Automating JIRA

Maximizing efficiency with minimum effort -- how to connect Cypress test results output to JIRA?

#2 Dario M. & Dean B. (Q Software): QA in Q

Dario will talk about testing online and POS payments as well as smartphone to smartphone money transfer for their Swiss based client Twint. Dean will talk about internal projects in Q software.

Lightning talks (5 minutes each)

#1 Tibor Kasas (Q Software): Riddles

Riddles may improve the way you think and it makes you think outside of the box. This is a quality that all QA engineers should pursuit.

#2 Davor Banovic (King ICT): Testing Machine Learning - bridging the gaps between chaotic and obvious.

Exploring ML Decision Tree Regression algorithm ability to resolve problems based on data. Converting concepts to tabular data. Demo with a card game in Python.
