Many software testers are there on Twitter tweeting daily about software testing. Why would one need a new social network, specially tailored for software testers? Few ideas comes to my mind. One is that it is convenient to have not so crowded place for chatting and quick and easy exchange of ideas among peers. @zeljkofilipin and myself ( @banovotz ) decided to create such network for all of you testers out there. Thx to shoutem , a great free service for creating social networks, we have testers.shoutem.com , a Twitter for software testers. Join us and lets have a test-up tweet-up on ViaQA 10.
