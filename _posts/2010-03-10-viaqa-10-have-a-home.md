![](http://testival.diotref.com/wp-content/uploads/2010/03/house.jpg?w=150 "Image: Francesco Marino / FreeDigitalPhotos.net")Ok, it is still not official, but have a roof over our heads. [Technology Development Centre from Osijek](http://www.tera.hr/ "Tera.hr")agreed to be a sponsor of ViaQA conference. We are talking conference room for 30+ people, few side rooms, computers, projector... ViaQA will also have a support regarding media contacts and spreading the word about our event through IT companies in Croatia. Isn't it wonderful? Thank you TERA :)
