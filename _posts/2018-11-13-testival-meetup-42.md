Introduction (about 10 minutes total)

At the beginning of every meetup, we like to get to know each other a bit better. Don't be shy and prepare to say a sentence or two about yourself.

Regular talks (10-30 minutes each)

#1 Vanja Zunic (Bornfight): The Evolving Idea of QA Fortress

Vanja Zunic, still a young and fresh, but amusingly enthusiastic guy interested in structured building, collaboration, processes, exploration, performance, accessibility, puzzles, mysteries and so on - currently working as a software tester, steel node of QA department at Bornfight.

#2 Vienne Chan: Testing Thinking

Looking at society as a system, can we apply a tester's way of thinking to solve some of its problems? This talk will present a hyper-speculative model in which a socio-economic system can be "hacked".

Vienne Chan is an artist visiting from Duisburg (Germany), and is interested in how to transform the financial system.

#3 Mladen Maravic (Repsly): Quality enforcement (QE), a QAs evil twin

What was it like to work for a company that puts quality first? Mladen will talk about his experience of working in a more rigorous development environment where Quality Enforcement was in power.
