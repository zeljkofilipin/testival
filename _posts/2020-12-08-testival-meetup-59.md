The many flavors of QA (45 min)

The complexity of the process determines how colorful the QA will be. The bigger the process, the more interesting the QA. Among other reasons, each part of a process has unique challenges that need to be addressed. Every team member is responsible for the quality and contributes in a different way and form. In Microblink, we have different QA processes and independently, yet together, we deliver quality products.
