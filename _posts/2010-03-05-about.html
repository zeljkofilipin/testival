What

Testival is, simply put, a software testers' event. A place for software testers to meet with each other and talk about testing. Testival meetup happens every month and Testival conference happens once a year. The conference is a combination of unconference format, where participants selects topics of interest, lightning talks and keynote speakers.

Where

So far Testival was in Osijek (2010, 2013, 2022), Zagreb (2011, 2014), Vara~din (2012), Split (2015), Rijeka (2016), Zadar (2017), Vukovar (2018) and Zabok (2019).
