[Meetup](https://www.meetup.com/SoftwareTestingClub/events/153781072/) (10)

We are announcing Zagreb Software Testing Club meet up #13 that will be held on Monday 9.12.2013, from 18.00. This time our host is Wispear Systems, Radnicka cesta 47, Zagreb.

If you would like to here about their open QA job opportunities and challenges in testing product from Wi-Fi telecommunication security domain, come and join us.
To certify or not to certify? That is the question that we here a lot at our meet ups. Second presentation will be experience report on BBST foundations AST certification course.

Sponsor of this meet up is our host, Wispear Systems, so after presentation we will discuss hot and not so hot testing topics over traditional free pizza and refreshments.
