Regular talks (10-30 minutes)

#1 Karlo Smid, Checklist: How to Generate Test Ideas in Agile Environment (45+15) min

Resources about software testing craft come in great amount and tester in agile environment could easily become overwhelmed. Result is poor testing under pressure where product and tester hurt. I will present a checklist of test ideas generated by community and triggered by Ministry of Testing and SmartBear (Fiddler creators). This is not checklist that will give you detailed test instructions in form of test cases. It is collection of test reminders and idea generators that will help you to start finding important bugs in agile environment. Every test idea will be supported with real world examples from my testing journey.
