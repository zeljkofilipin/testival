It is summer time and we should do this meetup in informal way. We will play some games connected with software testing. We will play:

- Fluxx - card game in which rules change from turn to turn. Batman edition.
- Set - card game in which you can enhance your pattern recognition capabilities
- TestSphere - find a lonely tester, approach her/him with one of test Sphere card. Rest is history.

Sponsor for this meetup is Tentamen.
