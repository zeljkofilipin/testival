[![Fakultet organizacije i informatike](http://testival.diotref.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/03/foi11.png "Fakultet organizacije i informatike")](http://www.foi.hr/eng) A few people from [FOI](http://www.foi.hr/eng) (Fakultet organizacije i informatike) have proposed a round table titled Importance of quality (systems) for the IT profession:
- [prof. dr. sc. Zdravko Krakar](http://www.foi.hr/djelatnici/zdravko.krakar): Quality and maturity of the IT profession, and different IT views (IT as practice; IT in business systems; IT in Croatia) - 30 minutes
- [doc. dr. sc. Melita Kozina](http://www.foi.hr/djelatnici/melita.kozina): SPI (Software Process Improvement) in software development process - 20 minutes
- [doc. dr. sc. Valentina Kirinić](http://www.foi.hr/djelatnici/valentina.kirinic): Software quality norms - 20 minutes
- [mr. sc. Nadica Hrgarek](http://at.linkedin.com/in/nhrgarek): Software verification and validation best practices - 20 minutes
- [prof. dr. sc. Zdravko Krakar](http://www.foi.hr/djelatnici/zdravko.krakar) (chair): Discussion - 30 minutes

More information on speakers and topics coming soon. **Update:** [Slides](https://docs.google.com/present/edit?id=0AUEvoPk1PsafZGR4enp2MzlfODMwY2dza2ozZDM&hl=en_US) are available.
