= **Regular talks** (10-30 minutes each)

**” Irja Straus: Beyond Pass and Fail**

Have you ever wondered what success means in the context of a test automation project? For me, success has been closely connected to the role I’m in. As a tester, success for me is simply pushing a test into CI/CD and seeing it pass and fail for the first time. As a test lead, for me, success is the information that each test reveals. While for me, as a manager, success is neither one nor the other - success is achieving results within a given budget.  
In this talk, I will share how I reached the “definition for success” of automation in testing, explain how we usually measure the value, and leave you with at least one practical metric that can be used in your own (successful) projects.  
\*This is my new talk I prepared and gave recently on Test Automation Days. Sharing it with you will give me valuable feedback, and open the discussion about our favorite thing - metrics. :)

Irja came into testing by accident, and after a successful product management adventure, she returned to it - intentionally. After 15+ years of developing skills in different companies and industries (SaaS, CPaaS, robotics, machine learning, AI, and U.S. healthcare), Irja now runs her own quality consulting company. She gave her first public talk at Testival (Zagreb STC, as we called it back then) exactly 10 years ago.
