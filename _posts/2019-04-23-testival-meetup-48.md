For our next meetup, we have invited a special guest from New York - Tim Lister!

With over 40 years of professional software development experience, Tim spends most of his time consulting on the front end of projects: project planning, project estimating and staffing, risk management, requirements definition, and project strategizing.

We are very excited about having Tim in our community.

The Meetup will be a great opportunity for some Q&A and meet & greet with Tim.

This meetup is organized in cooperation with KING ICT's Change Con and Zagreb Innovation Center -- ZICER! Thanks to this joint venture, you'll be able to learn and meet this world-class expert.
