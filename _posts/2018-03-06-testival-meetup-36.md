[Meetup](https://www.meetup.com/testival/events/247839886/)

Introduction (5-10 minutes)

At the beginning of every meetup, we get to know each other. Be prepared to say a sentence or two about yourself. If you are looking for a job or hiring, this would be a good time to mention it.

Regular talks (10-30 minutes)

#1 Davor Banović: Acceptance test driven development. Is it a myth?

This talk will cover the basic concepts of Acceptance test driven development, it's benefits and pitfalls. The talk is also a short introduction to RobotFramework. It includes some real stories and examples.

Davor has a dozen years of experience in software testing, test automation, customer support and team leading. He is founder and one of organisers of the Testival conference. Davor is QA Team Lead at Oradian.

Lightning talks (5 minutes)

#1 Karlo Smid: What I learned on European Testing Conference 2018
#2 Marko Kruljac: Practice your testing skills by playing board games
