Introduction (about 10 minutes total)

At the beginning of every meetup, we like to get to know each other a bit better. Prepare to say a sentence or two about yourself.

Regular talks (10-30 minutes each)

#1 Endrina Eskić (Software Tester @ Infinum): How I became friends with Gutenberg

Through my talk, I will tell you about the definition of WordPress and various aspects of WordPress that a software tester should know. I will also talk about the challenges in WordPress testing and try to clarify what everyone should keep in mind when they are responsible for a WordPress project.

#2 Irja Straus (Senior Quality Control Analyst / Team Leader @ Infobip): Using Requirements as Call to... Conversations

Testing begins even before a single line of code is written. This talk will show how I reinvented the wheel and learned that fact through product management experience, which helped me to contribute now as a tester by utilizing critical thinking at the right time during the product definition.
