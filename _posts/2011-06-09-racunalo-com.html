--- layout: post title: Racunalo.com date: 2011-06-09 21:22:52 author: zeljko.filipin categories: - 2011 --- Racunalo.com portal (ra unalo is computer in Croatian) is media sponsor of viaqa 11. They were a sponsor last year too. Racunalo.com is indepentent ICT news portal following the newest events in computers, communications, multimedia and business. It is founded in 1997 following the model of the first Croatian ICT radio show Radio Ra unalo (meaning Radio Computer in Croatian). Radio Ra unalo received bronze microphone award in 2006 at 10th Croatian Radio Festival and 12th Days of Radio in Croatia. The award was in the strongest category, Radio Accomplishments. The quality of Racunalo.com portal is proved by long and successful collaboration with numerous leading ICT, communications and media companies in Croatia.
