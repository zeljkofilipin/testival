[Meetup](https://www.meetup.com/testival/events/251062041/)

Introduction (about 10 minutes total)

At the beginning of every meetup, we get to know each other. Be prepared to say a sentence or two about yourself. If you are looking for a job or hiring, this would be a good time to mention it.

Regular talks (10-30 minutes each)

#1 Ana Prpic (Oradian), Ivana Maleš Galić (Span): Romanian Testing Conference 2018 Report

They will give a short report about Romanian Testing Conference 2018 and share some tips how to travel from Zagreb and Osijek to Cluj-Napoca. They will also share their thoughts, whether this conference met their expectations, how to select the right workshops and sessions, and how to prepare for workshops.

#2 Renato Ćurić (Comsysto Reply): e2e testing with TestCafe and Cucumber.js

TestCafe is a Node.js tool to automate end-to-end web testing. Cucumber.js is a JavaScript implementation of Cucumber and it is used for running automated tests written in plain language. With these 2 tools we can develop automated tests, build reports while staying true to business logic written in plain language.

Lightning talks (5 minutes each)
