[Meetup](https://www.meetup.com/SoftwareTestingClub/events/226163232/) (11)

It's time for Zagreb STC #23!

On this meetup Lada, Tesa and Gordana will present us how they cope with the challenge of proper communication with clients of their medical application.

Some of the interesting topics that will be covered are:

- Means of collecting client application requirements (phone, conference, workshop, web forms)
- How to filter those requirements
- How to identify "best customer"
- How to select "pilot customers"

We are sure that this presentation will sparkle a lot of interesting discussions!
