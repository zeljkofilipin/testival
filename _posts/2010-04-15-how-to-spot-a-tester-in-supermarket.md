Who does not like computer comics? Well, ok, I will rephrase the question, who among computer people does not like computer comics? [Userfriendly](http://www.userfriendly.org/), [Dilbert](http://www.dilbert.com/)... it is so great when you find a funny cartoon of situation you've been in and you miss the words to describe it. Testers found themselves oftenly in such situations. Cartoonwise, testers do not need to blend in general IT crowd any more.[Andy Glover](http://twitter.com/cartoontester), the [cartoontester](http://cartoontester.blogspot.com/) fills in not so crowded niche of software testing cartoons with his funny and witty cartoons about everyday situations in life of a software tester. ViaQA 10 conference is proudly hosting exhibition of selected Andy's cartoons in high resolution.
