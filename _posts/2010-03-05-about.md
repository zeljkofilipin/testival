## What
Testival is, simply put, a software testers' event. A place for software testers to meet with each other and talk about testing. Testival meetup happens every month and Testival conference happens once a year. The conference is a combination of unconference format, where participants selects topics of interest, lightning talks and keynote speakers. ![](http://deghq.com/wordpress/testival/wp-content/uploads/2010/03/IMG_20160924_114536-1024x768.jpg)
## Where
So far Testival was in Osijek (2010, 2013, 2022), Zagreb (2011, 2014), Varaždin (2012), Split (2015), Rijeka (2016), Zadar (2017), Vukovar (2018) and Zabok (2019). ![](http://deghq.com/wordpress/testival/wp-content/uploads/2010/03/IMG_20160923_184354-1024x768.jpg)&nbsp; ![](http://deghq.com/wordpress/testival/wp-content/uploads/2010/03/IMG_20160924_131854-1024x768.jpg)&nbsp; ![](http://deghq.com/wordpress/testival/wp-content/uploads/2010/03/IMG_20160923_194812-1024x768.jpg)
