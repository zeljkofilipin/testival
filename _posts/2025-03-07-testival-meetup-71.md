= **Regular talks** (10-30 minutes each)

**” Luka Vladika, End-to-End Testing Redefined: The Testa Platform**

As the number of apps, markets, and features grows at Happening, so does the complexity of testing them. That is why we're developing Testa: a unique platform that transforms how QA teams automate, execute, and debug end-to-end tests for web and mobile applications.  
With a seamless browser-based interface, real-device support, and advanced debugging capabilities, Testa eliminates traditional testing complexities and enables faster and more efficient test cycles.  
Join us to find out how Testa makes end-to-end testing faster, easier, and more efficient than ever before.

Luka is one of the Testa authors with a passion for automation and building QA tools.

**ČDinko Vranjicic, Load testing 101**

Applications and IT systems are becoming increasingly complex, driven by the growing demand for more features, content, and personalization. This ever-expanding need for data requires platforms to not only handle larger traffic volumes but also withstand unexpected surges (think Black Friday for any retailer).

At Happening, delivering a seamless customer experience means managing hundreds of updates per second concurrently without compromising performance. And, being a customer-centric company, our commitment is to ensure a smooth experience by any means necessary.  
Join us to explore the fundamentals of load testing and discover how you can jumpstart your performance testing journey with k6, which we utilized to discover the traffic limits of our APIs.

Dinko Vranjicic is a Senior QA Engineer at Happening. He has spent the past four years with the Offer team, making sure the latest data updates get to consumer-facing applications seamlessly, while also supporting the internal trading team with UI applications updates. Over his career, he’s worked with a variety of products, platforms, and technologies but feels most at home in the world of microservices, logs, and APIs. When he’s not tackling tech challenges, Dinko is a proud dad to two girls who keep him on his toes. He loves unwinding with long walks, cycling, movies, and good music.
