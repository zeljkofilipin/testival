[Meetup](https://www.meetup.com/SoftwareTestingClub/events/167263192/) (17)

We are announcing Zagreb Software Testing Club meet up #14 that will be held on Wednesday 26.02.2014, from 18.30. This time our host and sponsor is CloudSense, Radnicka cesta 80, Zagreb.

CloudSense will introduce their business and present their everyday challenges in software testing.

After the presentation we will discuss in unconference manner, hot and not so hot testing topics over traditional free pizza and refreshments.
