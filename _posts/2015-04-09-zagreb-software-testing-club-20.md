[Meetup](https://www.meetup.com/SoftwareTestingClub/events/221189114/) (12)

A mind map is a diagram used to visually organize information, typically created around a central concept with branching ideas.

Iva will explain how she incorporates mind mapping into her daily work. Attendees will have the opportunity to practice creating mind maps hands-on, as materials like crayons and notebooks will be provided for participants to work through real examples during the meeting.
