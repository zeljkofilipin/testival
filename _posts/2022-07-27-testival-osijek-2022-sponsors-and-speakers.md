## Speakers

We are also thrilled to announce 2 keynote speakers

![](https://testival.eu/wp-content/uploads/2022/07/Marie.webp)

[**Marie Drake**](https://www.mariedrake.com/)

Marie is currently a Quality Engineering Manager at Zoopla, she is based in the UK and she is also a Cypress Ambassador, an accessibility advocate, and an online course instructor at the Ministry of Testing and Test Automation University.

![](https://testival.eu/wp-content/uploads/2022/07/MotMirza.678e56f3.jpg)

[**Mirza Šiaić**](https://mirzasisic.com/)

Mirza is the organizer and founder of the Ministry of Testing Sarajevo community meetup.

He has a lengthy list of contributions to the testing community and industry.

Both have many years of testing experience and a wealth of knowledge so we are very excited to hear from them during the Testival conference!
