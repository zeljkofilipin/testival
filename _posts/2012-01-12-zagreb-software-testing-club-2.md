[Meetup](https://www.meetup.com/SoftwareTestingClub/events/45216182/) (10)

We are happy to announce Zagreb STC MeetUp #2 event that we will organize on Thursday, January 12, 2012, 5:30 PM at the Hvar conference room in Ericsson Nikola Tesla, Krapinska 45 Zagreb. For event opening, we can confirm 30 min light talk with demonstration of Grinder, a Java Load Testing Framework. We welcome anyone who may want to do a 5-minute lightning talk (that will trigger the discussion about any testing topic). In addition, we are looking for event sponsors that would provide the event with refreshments and snacks. For any info, please contact Karlo Smid at karlo(dot)smid(at)gmail(dot)com.
